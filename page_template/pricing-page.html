Billing FAQ

Can I change my plan later? expand_more
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the price difference will be prorated for your current billing cycle.
What payment methods do you accept? expand_more
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and for Enterprise customers, we also support bank transfers and ACH payments.
Is there a discount for non-profits? expand_more
Yes, we offer a 15% discount for verified 501(c)(3) non-profit organizations. Please contact our support team with your documentation to apply.
What happens if I exceed my product limit on the Starter plan? expand_more
You'll receive a notification when you're approaching your limit. If you need to add more products, you'll simply need to upgrade to the Professional plan to continue adding new items.
